Opportunity:

CHURCH OUTDOOR SPACE COMMUNITY CONNECTION

After investigating the church outdoor spaces, it was evident that they have the same issues that most churches around New Zealand have, which is the physical and spiritual disconnection from the secular society.

The question that was raised 'How can we socialize churchyards within a secular society?' which is to create a space where it can facilitate social cohesion for both the church community and the surrounding community of Whanganui.

  • Facilitate community cohesion between the church communuity and the surrounding community of Whanganui.
  • Create an inviting space for other users and to make them feel welcome within the church outdoor spaces.
  • Promote outdoor social engagement for the church community and the surrounding communities.
