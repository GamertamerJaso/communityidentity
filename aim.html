The Aim

Churchyards can facilitate community cohesion

The aim for this research is to understand how churchyards facilitate community cohesion between the church community and the surrounding community of Whanganui

The secondary aim is to explore how community engagement creates a sense of collective ownership and a sense of landscape identity of churchyards.
